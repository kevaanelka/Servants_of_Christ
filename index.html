Who We Are

A community
built on faith

Servants of Christ is a non-denominational Christian community rooted in Jakarta and Bekasi. We are not defined by walls or institutions — we are defined by our love for Jesus and for one another.

"Different backgrounds, different churches. But one Saviour and one source of truth."

We gather to read the Scripture together, build genuine community, and carry the Gospel outward — to our neighbours, our city, and beyond. Amazing to see how hungry our generation is for a community that glorifies Jesus.

✦ Today's Verse

01 Matthew 9:37  ·  Primary Foundation

"Then He said to His disciples, 'The harvest is plentiful, but the workers are few.'"

This is the heartbeat of our community. Jakarta and Bekasi are full of people who have yet to hear of Christ's love. We are called to be those workers — available, willing, and sent into the harvest.

02 Galatians 1:10  ·  Our Identity

"Am I now trying to win the approval of human beings, or of God? If I were still trying to please people, I would not be a servant of Christ."

To serve Christ is to forsake the pull of human approval. This verse names who we are — people who have chosen God's applause over the world's, free from the fear of man.

How We Live

The shape of our community

Scripture-First

Every gathering is grounded in God's Word. We open the Bible together and let it speak — no performance, no pretence, just the Text and the Spirit.

Non-Denominational

Different backgrounds, different churches — but one Saviour and one source of truth. Christ alone is our centre. All are welcome, whatever tradition you come from.

Authentic Fellowship

We bersekutu, memuji Tuhan, saling mendoakan — we fellowship, worship, and pray for each other. Beyond programmes, real friendships in the ordinary days of life.

Gospel Outreach

From street outreach (CFD Jakarta) to individual conversations and visiting nursing homes — we carry the Good News to those who haven't yet heard it.

Social Events

Community is a life shared — not just a weekly meeting. Meals, celebrations, nursing home visits, and the simple joy of being together beyond Sunday.
