Ambient audio samples
======================

Source: "Music Jingles" pack by Kenney Vleugels (www.kenney.nl)
License: CC0 1.0 Universal (Public Domain Dedication)
https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

Four individual plucked-string ("pizzicato") notes were selected from
the pack (A3, C4, D4, E4 — an A minor pentatonic fragment) and are
played back in a randomized sequence to create an original ambient
melody for the website. No attribution is legally required under CC0,
but it's credited here as good practice.
